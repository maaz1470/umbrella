(S.I. No. 535 of 2003) regarding protection code of personal data; Directive 2000/31/EC Regulations 2003, regarding electronic commerce.

The personal information will be exclusively required in the cases in which the information is necessary to identify and contact the user. In particular, the personal data will be gathered and conserved for the supply of our services and to supply the Judicial Authority with the required information; the personal data will be elaborated with the purpose of defining the users commercial profile which will be utilized by Pink Umbrella for its own marketing and promotional purposes.

The conferring of the consent of the treatment of the personal data, supplied for the above purposes, is necessary. Pink Umbrella will not be able to supply its services in the case in which the user refuses to agree to the above. Pink Umbrella, through the data gathering and conservation system, ensures and guarantees that this will not comprise, directly or indirectly, arguments regarding the sensitive data; therefore, any data treatment which may concern directly or indirectly sensitive data will be excluded before hand.

Furthermore, in Pink Umbrella portal, information on the pages visited by the user,within the Pink Umbrella portal, will be gathered. The data regarding the visited pages will never be connected to the users personal information. In the case in which the user gives his consent, his/her data could be used for the promotion of Pink Umbrella activities.

Pink Umbrella may only reveal the personal information if it is obliged to do so by law or in the case in which there is the firm conviction that such action is necessary in order to: (A) abide by the law (B) protect and defend t he rights or the property of Pink Umbrella.
