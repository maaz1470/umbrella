Tour Description

Join our original scavenger hunt for families and discover the highlights of the Eternal City in a fun way!

We have designed this unique outdoor treasure hunt led by a hunt master, who will also be your expert local guide. Listen carefully to their hints, solve riddles, answer quizzes and spot the clues scattered around the city: the more points you accumulate, the higher the chance to win the final prize! This is a great activity for families with children, it will stimulate the curiosity of your little ones and allow them to learn about history and art in an age-appropriate way. This is an interactive and fun tour!

You will embark on a thrilling adventure and learn all the secrets of Rome as you admire monuments such as the majestic Column of Marcus Aurelius, the Temple of Hadrian and the world-famous Trevi Fountain. Listen to fascinating anecdotes regarding the Pantheon and its dome, then take a walk through the iconic Piazza di Spagna and Piazza Navona, characterized by the stunning Fountain of the Four Rivers by Bernini. Create many lasting memories and spend some quality time with your children: this is a once-in-a-lifetime experience!

Our special tour is also highly customizable according to your age, preferred itinerary, availability and interests.
