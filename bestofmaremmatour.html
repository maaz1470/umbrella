Tour Description

Medieval towns, Spas. natural parks and wonders in Sothern Tuscany!

After visiting the art cities of central Italy like Rome or Florence, you need to immerse in the gentle and flavourful countryside .

This trip can start from Rome Florence or anywhere in the region you prefer. It can be your accommodation, the cruise port or the airport

Our driver with comfortable new vehicle (car, minivan or bus, depending on how many you are) will pick you up.

The tour includes the most famous and interesting sites of the Maremma area plus accommodation. You will visit Grosseto, Capalbio, Pitigliano, and the town of Scansano, where a popular wine is produced: the " Morelllino ". You will have the chance to visit a vineyard and taste the Morellino.

The region is rich of Spas and thermal waters so you will stop in Saturnia to enjoy the famous hot waterfalls .

You will enjoy not only breathtaking countryside sceneries but also colorful seaside villages like Porto Santo Stefano and Porto d'Ercole . Lay down on soft sandy beaches and amazing cliffs over the sea.

Since everyone has different preferences food will be recommended but not prearranged by your tour guide. She/he will recommend delicious local specialties such as pappardelle, tyical cheeses and salami, wild game, soups etc... etc...

The tour is minimum two days long. But if you have time we suggest to make it longer up to 7 days. There is a lot to see and experience in Maremma, and it is also a great place for relax

Don’t hesitate to contact us for information and bookings: info@pinkumbrella.com
