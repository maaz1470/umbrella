Tour Description

Discover the incredible ruins of Pompeii!

Discover the archaeological ruins of Pompeii on this 2.5-hour private tour as you take a fascinating journey back to the Roman Empire days with an expert, local guide.

Meet your guide at the entrance and enjoy skip-the-line admission tickets as you make your way into the UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Learn about how Pompeii was buried under volcanic ash for over 2,000 years following the eruption of Mt Vesuvius in 79 AD. See with your own eyes how the ash preserved the city and gain insight into how the inhabitants lived due to the extraordinary remains. Your guide will show you houses and everyday items and natural body casts that were almost intact when the city was unearthed.

Visit the small theatre where comedies and tragedies took place on the stage and the Roman Amphitheatre.

Walk over to see the glamourous thermal baths, which were like an ancient version of a health club with dressing rooms, baths, toilets and a public gymnasium.

You'll stop to see the fresco-covered walls of Vetti’s House, the Lupanare brothel and the Forum. Along the way you can hear the history of each site, as well as the full story behind the fateful day when the prosperous city was destroyed.

Pompeii offers a unique experience that is sure to amaze you!
